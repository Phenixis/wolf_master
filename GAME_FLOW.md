# Game flow

## Day 0

1. Cupid selects 2 players to be lovers. If one of them dies, the other dies too. If the lovers are on different teams, they win together if they are the last ones alive.

2. The mayor is elected. The mayor is able to break ties during voting. When the mayor dies, the mayor selects a new mayor.

## Night 1

1. The Seer wakes up and selects a player. The Seer learns if the player is a werewolf or not.
2. The werewolves wake up and select a player to kill.
3. The witch wakes up and learns who the werewolves killed. The witch can choose to save the victim or not, only once per game. The witch can also choose to kill another player, only once per game. The witch can only use one of her powers per night. If the witch used both powers, this step is skipped.

## Day 1

1. The players learn who the werewolves killed during the night.
2. The players discuss and vote to lynch a player. The player with the most votes is lynched. In case of a tie, the mayor breaks the tie.

## Night 2

Same as night 1.
